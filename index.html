About this tour

Embark on a cultural and spiritual journey! Discover Laotian temples and historical structures of various eras throughout Luang Prabang, marvel at the spectacular wonders of nature amid holy caves and three-tier waterfalls, taste exotic jungle fruits and local cuisine, and fully immerse yourself into the different ways of Laotian living.

Hotel: Parasol Blanc  starstarstarstar

Stylish and comfortable, the Parasol Blanc Hotel is a blend of traditional Laotian design with contemporary decor. Your Classic Room features a choice of king or twin beds, with a private balcony overlooking a lush tropical garden, and includes wireless internet, air-conditioning, mini-bar and complimentary bottled water.
